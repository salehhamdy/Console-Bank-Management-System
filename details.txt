Notes and Time Complexity
LinkedList Operations:

Insertion at End: O(1)
Display: O(N)
Stack Operations:

Push: O(1)
Pop: O(1)
Display: O(N)
Queue Operations:

Enqueue: O(1)
Dequeue: O(1)
Display: O(N)
Tree Operations:

Insertion: O(log N) on average
Search: O(log N) on average
Deletion: O(log N) on average
